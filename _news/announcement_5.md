Two papers accepted at **CVPR'26**: [OraPO](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.18600), a data-efficient reinforcement finetuning method for radiology report generation that achieves SOTA performance while only using less than 0.1% of training data, and a flow matching-based scene graph generation method.
